Translate

The Translate dialog, shown below, offsets the coordinates of selected or all TS program instructions by specified values.

Select Tools > Translate... to open it.

Here are descriptions of its components:

  • X - Horizontal offset, a positive or negative integer.
  • Y - Vertical offset, a positive or negative integer.
  • Translate selection - When checked, the dialog translates only the coordinates of the segment of the TS program currently selected. Otherwise, it translates the coordinates of the entire program.
  • Translate - Offsets the coordinates of selected or all TS program instructions by the specified values.
  • Cancel - Dismisses the dialog.

Tools > Translate to Center horizontally translates the entire TS program such that the y-axis passes through the middle of the resultant circuit.
